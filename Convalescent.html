If you are fully recovered from COVID-19, you may be able to help other patients currently fighting the infection by donating your convalescent plasma. Because you fought the infection, your plasma now contains COVID-19 antibodies. These antibodies provided one way for your immune system to fight the virus when you were sick, so your plasma may be able to be used to help others fight off the disease.

Unlike regular blood donation, plasma donors tend to donate about a half litre of blood for the transfusion with the help of a single-use disposable apheresis kit. Once the plasma donation is done for, the machine only retains the plasma and return all red blood cells and other components of blood.
